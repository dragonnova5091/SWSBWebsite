Our story

Built by a small business, for small businesses.

Dragon's Quill started in 2026 by a developer and his wife. Ethan was tired of seeing businesses with no online information, so he started Dragon's Quill. Here we seek to forge a relationship with our clients, focusing on long term collaboration over short term monetization.

Mission

Provide businesses with online information and increased visibility.

At Dragon's Quill we strive to provide a website for small businesses in need. We seek to build relationships with our clients that will last for years, all while providing them with the tools they need to succeed.
